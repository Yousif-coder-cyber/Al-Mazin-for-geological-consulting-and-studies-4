About Us

Al-Mazin Bureau for Geological Studies and Consulting was established in (1998) and includes a distinguished group of consultants in various fields of geology, many of whom have worked for years in relevant government departments and institutions. The Bureau’s future vision goes beyond the traditional role of consulting firms, aiming to develop ambitious strategic plans to position Iraq in the place it rightfully deserves.
